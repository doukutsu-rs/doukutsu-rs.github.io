We're excited to announce the release of doukutsu-rs 0.102.0-beta7!

The most notable changes in this release are:

- We've got a new website and release system! All releases are now available on [https://get.doukutsu.rs](https://get.doukutsu.rs)
- The app icons have been updated to new cute crabby Sue art by AppleHair from doukutsu-rs Discord server.
- doukutsu-rs is now available on Flathub! https://flathub.org/apps/io.github.doukutsu_rs.doukutsu-rs
- The version label of previous Android release was broken due to a typo in our CI workflow script. This has been fixed.

For a complete list of changes, see the full changelog: [https://github.com/doukutsu-rs/doukutsu-rs/compare/0.101.0-beta6...0.102.0-beta7](https://github.com/doukutsu-rs/doukutsu-rs/compare/0.101.0-beta6...0.102.0-beta7)

We appreciate all the contributions and look forward to continuing to improve doukutsu-rs!
